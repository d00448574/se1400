What about competition?

My goal of this site is to display my knowledge and work to potential employers or coworkers, and to show my work. In the process of creating this website, I came across many other portfolios and used them as stepping stones to create mine. I saw many amazing sites, but I found a common theme. Some were too difficult to navigate for me specifially, which caused frustration. However, their target audience might be future employers, in which case, they would be able to navigate more efficiently. I want my personal site to be easy to use for both newcomers and experienced alike, and want to find fun but simple ways to showcase the journey that I have been on.
